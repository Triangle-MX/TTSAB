About the Proyect...

By using a drone and a 3D camera, students record and share virtual science tours. Everybody can live an extraordinary experience in 3D by using Cardboards and their own smartphone. Students record videos and upload them into a specific app to share with others.

The objective of this project is to have students produce a series of bilingual Science videos to share with others, in our school and in the web. Mac computers, iPads, drones, smartphones and Cardboards are some devices we use to share with the school community. Students will be able to model information, to capture images, to shoot videos and scientific content in order to learn and create a knowledge society from an early age

The way that young students can model and create their own instruments and learn how they can explore and build a new knowledge society with a social responsibility to benefit themselves and others, only by using devices as iPads, iPods, 3D cameras, drones and computers. Imaginative activities in all academic subjects built for kids. Designing a number of useful activities through which students will be able to learn while having fun and the chance to solve daily problems without even noticing or reviewing previous knowledge.

This Project is based on images and 360 videos. Every single image and video has been uploaded to a website that can be visited. There you can view them on a smartphone, open them and see what we have been working in. The secret is to concentrate on the code while building the website, and then uploading the information, photos and videos. It is worth mentioning that this project is constantly improving and growing, incorporating observations and collaborations from the school community (classmates and other students at school). It is currently being used by the whole class (6 th grade), and we wish to expand it to other classes every year.
